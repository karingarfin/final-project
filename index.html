Why Small Theatre Matters

by Karin Garfin


For the first time the Mayor's Office of Media and Entertainment studied small live theater venues and their impact on our economy, society and culture. Titled: "ALL NEW YORK'S A STAGE: NYC Small Theater Industry Cultural and Economic Impact Study" the report outlines the some surprising facts.

During the announcement the Commissioner of the Mayor's Office of Media and Entertainment, Anne del Castillo explained the report is part of "an ongoing efforts to amplify the diversity of NYC theater as well as the media and entertainment industry, and shine a light on the cultural and economic contributions of small theaters to NYC's creative economy." The NYC Small Theater Industry Cultural and Economic Impact Study, provides an in-depth look at a little-understood but important component of New York City's cultural scene.
